Basketball


History

Basketball was invented in 1891 by James Naismith as an indoor winter sport using a ball and two peach baskets. It quickly spread across the U.S. and became an Olympic sport in 1936. The NBA formed in 1946, growing the game globally with iconic players over the decades. Today, it’s one of the world’s most popular sports. Learn More Here!

How To Play

Basketball is played between two teams of five. The goal is to score by shooting the ball through the opponent’s hoop while preventing them from doing the same. Players move the ball by dribbling or passing, and the team with the most points at the end of the game wins. Defense involves blocking shots, stealing the ball, and contesting passes.

Players on the Court

  • Point Guard
  • Shooting Guard
  • Small Forward
  • Power Forward
  • Center
